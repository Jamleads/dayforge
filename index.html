How to use guide

Up and running in 5 minutes

1

Make a copy of the sheet

Open the link and go to File → Make a copy. This saves it to your own Google Drive so only you can edit it.

Rename it something memorable — e.g. "DayForge 2026"
2

Fill in today's row

Column A = today's date. Column B = your energy out of 10. Columns C, D, E = your top 3 most important tasks.

Be honest with the energy rating — the pattern will surprise you over time.
3

Track your routines (Columns F–Q)

At the end of each day, mark each habit using the dropdown:

✓
Done
B
Break
S
Sick
✕
Missed
4

Check your daily score (Column R)

Column R auto-calculates how many routines you completed each day. No manual input needed.

Score What it means
10–12 Exceptional day — you showed up fully
7–9 Solid day — strong consistency
4–6 Average day — room to grow
0–3 Rough day — note what got in the way
5

Review your weekly analytics

At the end of each week, check the Analytics table. See your best day, worst day, average score, and completion %.

Ask yourself: what made my best day different? Do more of that.
6

Read (or listen to) the book summaries

Start with Eat the Frog if you struggle with procrastination. Atomic Habits if you want to understand why the system works. The Miracle Morning if your mornings feel chaotic.

7

Customise to fit your life

The Formula Guide tab explains every formula in plain language. Add habits, rename columns — no advanced spreadsheet skills needed.
